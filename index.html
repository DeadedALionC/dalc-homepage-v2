Deaded Anatomy Lion Club

DALC is a collection of 10,000 lions dissected through the organs to reveal the inner workings of the amazing underlying system AKA the community.

ABOUT THE ART

As a team, we put a lot of thought in producing this PFP NFT collection. This collection, which revolves around the King of the Jungle, is largely inspired by the founders’ place of residence, living in the Lion City. Coincidentally, the very first NFT bought by the founders were the CDC's Loaded Lions, hence the Loaded Lion family hold a special place in our hearts; the project, their achievements or the lack thereof all contributed to the vision/mission for the DALC.

Our art comprises of a humane lion half reminding us on the inherent good-natured and courageous souls which reside within. While in contrast, the "Deaded" aspect of the art aims to portray the uncertainty and devious nature of the NFT/metaverse which are highly unpredictable and can slowly eat away the trust and respect for the field. We aim to close this gap and provide a collection which is authentic, legitimate and most importantly valuable for our holders. And we pride ourselves in deploying these lions to protect and preserve the better half of field, essentially providing a safe space for our holders.

DALC'S LORE

The Lion has always been revered as the King of the Jungle, powerful creatures who are a symbol of courage and strength. Ferocious during combat, and steadfast in defence – they are the sword, the shield and most importantly the pride of the jungle who bring value to any community they pledge themselves to.

From the prosperous lion city state where technology is boundless, 10,000 individuals volunteered to the call for a venture into the wilderness of the blockchain. Needless to say, these young lions did not know what they were getting into since the “metaverse” was such a mystery to most. Mostly extravagant stories which were fed through the media of previous successful expeditions to the edge of the renowned “OpenSea” – which were enticing to most who lived deep in the dense vegetation and urbanization of the real-world. Yet little was heard of any negative side effects for traversing this said “metaverse”. It all sounded too good to be true, and there was only one way to find out.

With a flip of a finger, our young lions adopted their newfound Web3 identity before entering the bleep and into the Metaverse.

There were so many like-minded communities, clubs and societies co-existing here; and apes seemed to have dominated the field. Except many turned out to be a hoax as our lions would learn during their time there. It was difficult to trust anyone out here – to differentiate the nonchalant veteran apes from the many bad ape-ples trying to pull off as one. It was just such an unusual space – from punks who have found their place in society, to crooks who aren’t all that bad, and where real friends may be those whom you could never really see (eye to eye with). Yet hang around long enough to reveal the true colours of certain frosted individuals who soon pool to a puddle, bunnies that eventually rot to death, poor dwarves who remain short (of expectations), and lords who were dirtbags after all.

While the potential for growth is in abundance here on the blockchain, the same can be said for the dangers and threats that lurk within. Our young courageous lions would soon find themselves wasting away from the toxic environment hidden in the great Metaverse unbeknownst to most outside of it. The pack suffered a painless degradation over time which they have concluded would soon turn them into dust. These lions could not simply sit around and await their doom, they regrouped and unashamed of their experiences, embraced the lessons learnt that carved onto their very identity in search of their redemption. The 10,000 strong pack would soon move to establish their identity in the Metaverse as the Deaded Anatomy Lion Club (DALC).

The Metaverse will never be a safe place to be in, unless we make it to be – which is the mission of the DALC; to build a safe thriving haven here in the Metaverse.
